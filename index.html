Our Legacy

Since our establishment in 1964, Umarkhadi Consumers Cooperative Society Limited has been a cornerstone of the Mumbai community. Registered under Mumbai-Kanpur 146-1964, we've grown from a small cooperative to a thriving organization serving thousands of members.

Our commitment to cooperative principles and community service has remained unwavering for over six decades.
